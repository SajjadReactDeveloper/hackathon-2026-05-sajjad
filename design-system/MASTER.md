## Design Philosophy

**Data-dense, neutral palette.** FlowChat is a business cockpit for Pakistani SMB sellers — not a consumer app. Design priorities:
1. Information density over whitespace (sellers need to see many messages/orders at once)
2. Neutral palette with high contrast (usable in bright sunlight on mobile)
3. Urdu glyph quality (sidebar labels, toasts, and notifications may render in Urdu script)
4. Consistent sidebar + top bar across all pages

| `text-2xl` | 24px | 700 | Dashboard tile numbers |

Font stack: `Inter, "Noto Nastaliq Urdu", system-ui, sans-serif`

## Spacing Scale

4px base unit. Use multiples: `4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64px`.

## Component Patterns

### Dashboard tiles
- shadcn `Card` with `CardHeader` + `CardContent`
- Tile number: `text-2xl font-bold`
- Tile label: `text-sm text-muted-foreground`
- Trend indicator: green/red badge

### Inbox conversation item
- Avatar (initials, 40px) + contact name (`font-medium`) + preview (`text-muted-foreground text-sm truncate`)
- Unread count badge: `bg-primary text-primary-foreground rounded-full text-xs px-1.5`
- Active: `bg-accent`

### Message bubble
- Inbound: `bg-muted` left-aligned
- Outbound (AI): `bg-primary/10 border border-primary/20` right-aligned
- Audio message: waveform icon + duration + play button
- Timestamp: `text-xs text-muted-foreground`

### Status badges
- Order status: `pending` gray, `confirmed` blue, `shipped` indigo, `delivered` green, `cancelled` red
- Fraud score: 0-30 green, 31-60 yellow, 61-100 red

## Anti-patterns

- No drop shadows on cards (flat design)
- No rounded corners > `rounded-lg` (8px max)
- No gradients on data elements
- No emoji in committed code/copy
- No animations > 200ms on data-dense pages (distracting)
- No `overflow-hidden` on conversation list (breaks Urdu glyphs)
